About MetaFinOps

We're building the cost governance layer for the AI era. Founded in 2025, MetaFinOps is on a mission to make AI infrastructure costs visible, controllable, and sustainable.

Our Story

MetaFinOps began in 2025 as a vision to solve one of the biggest challenges in modern AI adoption: skyrocketing GPU and AI workload costs.

While working deeply in cloud engineering, FinOps, and data platforms, our founders recognized a massive gap: traditional FinOps tools could not measure or optimize the costs of AI, including GPUs, LLM tokens, model inference, fine-tuning, or GreenOps requirements.

This insight led to the founding of MetaFinOps. Today, we are building the first AI-native FinOps platform designed specifically for GPU cost attribution, AI workload optimization, token cost intelligence, and DevOps-integrated cost guardrails.

Innovation First

We push the boundaries of what FinOps can do. From GPU telemetry to carbon analytics, we build what doesn't exist yet.

Transparency Always

Costs should never be hidden. We believe every team deserves full visibility into what they spend and why.

Sustainability Matters

Cost optimization and carbon reduction go hand in hand. We build tools that help enterprises do both.
