THE CHALLENGE

Carbon Reporting Is Now Mandatory

EU and US regulations require Scope 2 & 3 emissions reporting from cloud infrastructure. Non-compliance means fines and reputational damage.

CIOs must show both cost savings and carbon reduction to the board. Today these are tracked in completely separate tools.

No existing FinOps tool combines cost and carbon intelligence into a single optimization engine with actionable recommendations.

OUR APPROACH

Unified Cost + Carbon Intelligence

MetaFinOps maps every cloud resource to both its dollar cost and its carbon footprint. One platform, one dashboard, one optimization strategy.

We ingest carbon intensity data from AWS, GCP, and Azure APIs, then correlate it with your actual resource usage to produce accurate, auditable emissions reports.

Our engine finds opportunities where reducing spend also reduces carbon, and highlights trade-offs where greener regions may cost slightly more but deliver significant emissions savings.
