Product Roadmap

Our 6-month MVP journey — from foundation to enterprise-ready AI-native FinOps platform.

Completed

Months 1–2: Foundation & Core Ingestion

  • Multi-cloud cost data ingestion (AWS, GCP, Azure)
  • Unified cost normalization and tagging engine
  • Basic dashboard with spend-by-service views
  • GPU instance identification and tracking
  • Authentication, RBAC, and tenant isolation
  • CI/CD pipeline and infrastructure scaffolding
In Progress

Months 3–4: Intelligence & Guardrails

  • GPU utilization monitoring with idle detection alerts
  • Token cost modeling for OpenAI, Anthropic, Vertex AI
  • PR cost annotation bot (GitHub & GitLab)
  • Terraform cost preview integration
  • Budget policy engine with threshold blocking
  • Anomaly detection with Slack/PagerDuty notifications
Planned

Months 5–6: GreenOps & Enterprise

  • Carbon footprint tracking per service, region, and GPU-hour
  • Green region recommendations for batch workloads
  • ESG compliance report exports (PDF & CSV)
  • Model-level cost attribution dashboards
  • Team-level budget management and approval workflows
  • SSO integration and enterprise admin controls
Planned

Q3 2025: Scale & Optimize

  • RI/SP discount optimization recommendations
  • Multi-model cost comparison engine
  • Custom policy builder (no-code rule creation)
  • Kubernetes cost allocation (namespace & pod level)
  • Advanced forecasting with ML-based spend prediction
  • Public API and webhook integrations
Planned

Q4 2025: Ecosystem & Marketplace

  • Integration marketplace (Datadog, Grafana, ServiceNow)
  • Custom dashboard builder with drag-and-drop widgets
  • FinOps-as-Code SDK for infrastructure-as-code workflows
  • Multi-tenant reseller portal for MSPs
  • SOC 2 Type II certification
  • AI-powered optimization copilot

Three Pillars of the Platform

Every milestone on our roadmap maps to one of three strategic pillars.

AI/GPU FinOps

GPU utilization tracking, token cost modeling, idle detection, model-level attribution, and right-sizing recommendations.

DevOps FinOps

PR cost annotations, CI/CD budget guardrails, Terraform preview, policy-as-code enforcement, and anomaly detection.

GreenOps

Carbon per GPU-hour, green region scheduling, ESG compliance exports, and sustainability-aware optimization.
