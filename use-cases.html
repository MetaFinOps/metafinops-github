Use Cases

Whether you're a lean AI startup, a multi-team enterprise, or a regulated FinTech — MetaFinOps adapts to your cost optimization needs.

AI Startups

Series A–C companies with growing GPU spend

You're burning through GPU credits faster than your runway allows. Every idle A100 hour is money that could fund another sprint. You need visibility into which models and experiments drive cost — without building internal tooling.

Key Pain Points

  • GPU instances left running after training jobs complete
  • No visibility into cost per model or experiment
  • Token costs spiraling across multiple LLM providers
  • Engineering team has no incentive to optimize

MetaFinOps Features

  • GPU idle detection with Slack/PagerDuty alerts
  • Per-model cost attribution dashboards
  • Token cost modeling across OpenAI, Anthropic, Vertex
  • Right-sizing recommendations for GPU instances

Enterprise

50+ teams, multi-cloud, governance-first

Your cloud bill exceeds seven figures monthly. You have dozens of teams, each with different budgets and usage patterns. You need policy-driven guardrails that scale without manual reviews, plus audit trails that satisfy finance and compliance.

Key Pain Points

  • Cost allocation across 50+ teams is manual
  • No automated budget enforcement at deployment time
  • Finance demands monthly cost-to-BU reports
  • ESG reporting requirements growing annually

MetaFinOps Features

  • Team-level budget policies with auto-enforcement
  • PR cost annotations for every engineering team
  • Cost-to-business-unit mapping and exports
  • Carbon reporting with ESG-ready compliance exports

FinTech / BFSI

Regulated industries with compliance requirements

Compliance and auditing are non-negotiable. Your regulators expect detailed cost allocation reports. Every infrastructure decision needs documentation, and your AI workloads must be traceable from deployment to cost center.

Key Pain Points

  • Regulatory audits require detailed cost lineage
  • AI model costs must map to specific business lines
  • Strict budget controls needed for compliance
  • Carbon footprint reporting mandated by ESG rules

MetaFinOps Features

  • Audit-ready cost lineage and tagging
  • Business-line cost mapping with approval workflows
  • Policy-as-code budget enforcement
  • SOC 2 and ESG compliance report exports
